Acme Corp Employee Handbook

Working Hours and Remote Work
Acme Corp follows a hybrid working model. Employees are expected to be present
in the office on Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday, and may work remotely on
Monday and Friday. Core collaboration hours are from 11:00 AM to 4:00 PM in the
employee's local time zone.

Leave Policy
Every full-time employee is entitled to 24 days of paid annual leave per year.
In addition, employees receive 12 days of sick leave. Annual leave must be
requested at least two weeks in advance through the HR portal. Unused annual
leave of up to 5 days can be carried over to the next calendar year.

Refund and Reimbursement Policy
Business expenses are reimbursed within 30 days of submitting a valid receipt.
Travel, meals during business trips, and approved software subscriptions are
all reimbursable. Reimbursement requests above 500 dollars require prior
manager approval.

Code of Conduct
All employees must treat colleagues with respect and maintain confidentiality
of customer data. Violations of the data protection policy may result in
disciplinary action up to and including termination.

Performance Reviews
Performance reviews are conducted twice a year, in June and December. Reviews
assess goal completion, collaboration, and technical impact. Promotions are
decided during the December review cycle.
